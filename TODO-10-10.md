## You Must Do

- Confirm Vercel is deploying the repository root, not the nested `meteoaumaroc/` folder.

### Search engines

- Add and verify both `https://meteoaumaroc.com` and `https://www.meteoaumaroc.com` in Google Search Console.

- Submit the live sitemap after deploy.

### Performance validation

- Run PageSpeed Insights on:
- homepage
- `/cities`
- 3 major city pages
- 3 guide pages
- Run Lighthouse in Chrome against the live domain, mobile and desktop.
- Monitor CrUX/Core Web Vitals over time; do not rely only on local lab builds.

### Business / policy / trust

- Decide whether your AdSense account structure is policy-safe for your business.

- Add social proof, update cadence, and editorial responsibility if you want to compete internationally.
- Keep legal pages accurate to actual tracking, consent, and advertising behavior.

## Success Criteria

You can call it "close to 10/10" only when all of this is true:

- Lighthouse desktop and mobile are consistently 90+ on key pages.
- Core Web Vitals are green in field data.
- All important pages are indexed on the correct host.
- City pages exist, render properly, and are crawlable.
- Internal linking is strong and guides rank for non-brand queries.
- Ads, analytics, consent, and legal text all match real live behavior.
- The site is stable after deploy and monitored.
